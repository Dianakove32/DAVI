Facebook to stop recommending civic and political groups

Facebook will stop recommending users join "civic and political" groups, as it tries to reduce the number of political posts in people's feeds. It follows weeks of suppressing such content around the US election but will now become permanent policy around the world. Mark Zuckerberg announced the change in a phone call with investors. "People don't want politics and fighting to take over," the Facebook boss told those on the call.
